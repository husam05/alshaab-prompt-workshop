The Prompt Anatomy — interactive

Hover or tap each part to see how it builds a complete, professional prompt. This structure produced every result below.

1Role · الدور
Who the AI acts as — expertise & perspective that shapes tone and depth.
2Task · المهمة
One precise action — design, generate, evaluate, mentor.
3Context · السياق
Course, audience, data, goal, region — grounds the output in reality.
4Output · المخرجات
Exact format & required sections (table, count, fields).
5Constraints · القيود
Limits, ethics, tone, word count, "no fabricated references".
